Overview

Purpose

The purpose of the is website is to create a database of plants that are in the greenhouse that can be searched. It has pictures and instructions for care for the plants.

Audience

Plant lovers of all kinds. Also new homeowners, and contractors fo their customers.

Dynamic elements

The plants page will have a array or database of the plants including a picture and the info about the plants. I want to make a search bar as well that lets you filter the plants based on some of parts of the description.

Home page

Welcome to the Greenhouse Guide! Here you will find a variety of plants with care instructions and images.

Here is a guide to what the information on the first page displays.

Watering: "Low" means minimal watering once established; "Moderate" means regular but not excessive watering. Adjust based on your local climate.
Height/Spread: Ranges reflect common cultivars; check specific varieties at your nursery.
Deer Resistance: "High" means rarely eaten, "Moderate" means occasionally browsed if food is scarce. No plant is fully deer-proof, but these are widely recognized as resistant.
Zones: These are USDA hardiness zones; ensure they match your location (or use as annuals if outside the range).
